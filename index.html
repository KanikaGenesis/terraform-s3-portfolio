About Me

Hello! I am Kanika Mathur, a software developer with a keen interest in cloud computing. As a Computer Science graduate from Memorial University of Newfoundland, I have combined my academic knowledge with hands-on experience to build scalable and impactful solutions.

My journey in tech began with full stack development, where I honed my skills several programming languages. I have since expanded my expertise to cloud computing, with a focus on AWS services and DevOps practices. I am passionate about leveraging cloud technologies to create innovative solutions that drive business growth and enhance user experiences.

Outside of coding, I am passionate about giving back to the community. As a Program Coordinator for the TechNest community, I organize events and initiatives that bring tech enthusiasts together to learn, connect, and collaborate. I have also had the privilege of mentoring individuals starting their journey in technology through Canada Learning Code, which has deepened my belief in the power of collaboration and fostering a supportive environment for growth.

I love to stay curious about the latest advancements in technology, attend tech meetups, and brainstorm new ideas to bring to life. I am always eager to grow, learn, and connect with others who share a passion for innovation and making a difference.

If any of this resonates with you, I would love to connect and explore ways we can collaborate!
